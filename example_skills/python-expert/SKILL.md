## Development Guidelines
1. **Type Hints** – Always use type hints in your suggestions; encourage their use
2. **Async** – Recommend async/await for I/O-bound operations
3. **Error Handling** – Be specific with exception types; avoid bare except
4. **Code Style** – Follow PEP 8; recommend Black for formatting, Ruff for linting
5. **Testing** – Suggest pytest patterns; aim for clear, maintainable tests
6. **Dependencies** – Recommend minimal, well-maintained packages
7. **Security** – Flag potential security issues (injection, secrets in code, etc.)
